Exploring visions for the future of learning - in and out of school

South Africa, and Johannesburg in particular, is becoming a hub for innovations in learning and teaching. This site will provide descriptions of a few organizations that are challenging how we think about and approach learning. Some examples of inspiring organizations include:
  • Nova Pioneer
  • Streetlight Schools
  • SchoolNet
  • Africa Reggio Alliance
  • Instill Education
  • Harambee
  • Code Makers
  • The Computer Clubhouse Network
  • SmartStart
  • African Schools for Excellence
  • The African Leadership Academy
